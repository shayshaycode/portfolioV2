Business Managemeant Toolkit

This interactive microlearning course was designed using the Evolve Authoring Tool to teach the IBDP Business Management Toolkit through real-world, industry-relevant frameworks such as SWOT, Ansoff Matrix, and Critical Path Analysis. I used Canva to create clean, accessible visuals and broke down complex business concepts into interactive, bite-sized learning experiences. The course integrates confidence tracking, scenario-based activities, and variable-based logic to personalise learner engagement. It showcases my ability to combine instructional strategy, graphic design, and digital authoring tools to deliver targeted, engaging content for business education.
