Singapore Drone Regulations Course

The Drone Pilot SG course was developed to educate drone enthusiasts and professionals on Singapore's regulatory environment for drone operations. Utilizing Articulate Storyline and custom web components (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript), this interactive course ensures compliance with local laws and enhances drone safety practices.

This project included creating interactive lessons, quizzes, scenario-based assessments, and integrating SCORM packages for seamless LMS compatibility.
