More About Me

Instructional Designer & Educator

Bio

As an experienced educator with a dynamic background in both psychology and business management, my career journey has taken me from the vibrant classrooms of Singapore to diverse international landscapes like Morocco, Switzerland, and Taiwan. My global experiences have enriched my teaching approach, allowing me to bring a unique perspective to education.

Over the past few years, I have been passionate about integrating e-learning into my courses, leveraging technology to create immersive and engaging learning experiences. I am a proficient coder, holding various certifications that attest to my technical skills. My expertise extends beyond the classroom, having collaborated with numerous clients to develop bespoke courses and innovative instructional materials.

Born and raised in the sunny state of Florida, I have a deep love for surfing, hiking, and exploring the great outdoors. These activities not only provide me with a sense of adventure but also inspire my creativity and problem-solving abilities.

I am always on the lookout for my next challenge, constantly learning, and thinking of new ways to creatively address problems using design and technology. My goal is to continue growing my expertise and to work harder than the rest to achieve excellence in instructional design.

Learning Philosophy

Dual-Process Synergy

I integrate dual-process theory into my designs, engaging both intuitive and analytical thinking. This creates learning experiences that are accessible yet intellectually robust.

Gamified Engagement

I infuse game mechanics and interactive challenges to make learning fun and motivating. This approach boosts participation and helps learners retain and apply new skills.

Simplicity in Design

I embrace minimalism—removing unnecessary complexity to create clean, intuitive interfaces. This focus on simplicity ensures that learners stay centered on core concepts.

Innovative Tools

By leveraging the latest, industry-leading tools and technologies, I push the boundaries of instructional design. This keeps the learning experience current, dynamic, and transformative.
